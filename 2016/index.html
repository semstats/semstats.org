SemStats, 2016 edition

This year, SemStats is again co-located with ISWC and thus will take place in Kobe, Japan, in October, the perfect time to admire the wonderful colors of the Japanese autumn leaves.

2016 is a year of important opportunities for SemStats with important projects combining statistics and semantics being launched by prominent international organizations: see the call for contributions for details.

Last but not least, the workshop benefits this year from the support of a sponsor, which will allow for distributing generous prizes to the best contributions.

So 2016 is the year for contributing to SemStats, but note that the deadline is July 715th 2016.
